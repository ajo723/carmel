About Carmel Giri School

Founded by St. Kuriakose Elias Chavara, our school fosters holistic education through intellectual, moral, and spiritual growth.

About the Exhibition

The Carmel Giri Exhibition 2024 showcases the creativity, hard work, and passion of our students. It's an opportunity to reflect the values of education and service taught by St. Chavara.

Our Facilities

Carmel Giri English Medium School provides a range of world-class facilities to ensure a holistic and enriching learning experience for every student. Our facilities are designed to support both academic excellence and the overall development of students.

Our Core Values

At Carmel Giri English Medium School, we strive to nurture students not only academically but also morally, spiritually, and culturally. Our values are rooted in the teachings of St. Kuriakose Elias Chavara, and we focus on shaping students into responsible, disciplined, and compassionate individuals.
